About Sawari

We're on a mission to transform how people experience public transportation by providing real-time, accurate, and accessible bus tracking information.

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by a team of transit enthusiasts and technology experts, Sawari was born out of frustration with unreliable bus schedules and long waiting times. We knew there had to be a better way to connect people with public transportation.

Today, we serve over 50,000 daily users across the city, providing real-time tracking for 25+ bus routes with 95% accuracy. Our technology has reduced average waiting times by 60% and helped thousands of people rely on public transport with confidence.
